Doorprac NG

Door sweep

Covers the space between floor and door

DOORPRAC NG is a profile that covers the space between door and floor, and avoids the entrance of dust, insects, mosquitos, hot and cold air currents, small animals and water spillings.

It is manufactured by a double extrusion process, where a rigid pvc profile and a flexible pvc profile are fused together.

This way, the flexible profile that sweeps the floor when opening and closing the door, remains glued to the rigid profile that is adhered to the door, avoiding the separation of the two profiles with the passing of time, like in the case of the aluminum door sweep, where the aluminum profiles holds the flexible pvc profile by a compressing action, that may loosen up with time.

DOORPRAC NG, comes with a double action adhesive tape, adhered in the back of the rigid PVC profile.

It is offered in 2 lengths, 2.94" and 3.92", and in 8 colors to combine or contrast with the color of the door.

The installation is easy: just measure the width of the door, choose the DOORPRAC NG in the appropriate length that exceeds the width of the door, cut it to that width, place it on the door, make sure the flexible profile rests over the floor but with out exaggerating the pressure over it, mark the position with a pencil, retrieve the red protective film of the tape, adhere the DOORPRAC NG on the door using as a guide the previous mark, pressing it with your hands all along the width of the door.
