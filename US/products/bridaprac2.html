Bridaprac II

Kit for toilet installation with Bridaprac

Includes everything for its installation

To make the complete installation of a toilet more easy, we offer the BRIDAPRAC II kit, that includes:

  • 1 Flexible hose of pvc with aluminum covering, 133⁄4" of length, with zinc alloy connectors of 1⁄2"×7⁄8".
  • 1 Zink alloy control valve of 1⁄2" × 1⁄2".
  • 1 Bridaprac: is a bridle of polyethylene with an extension of rubber to fit a 4 inch drain pipe.
  • 1 Screw set of 1⁄4" × 3", nut and ring of 1⁄4", plastic anchors of 1⁄4", and cover top for nuts.
  • 1 Teflon tape of 1⁄2" × 259".
