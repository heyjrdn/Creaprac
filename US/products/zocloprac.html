Zocloprac

Covebase for floor

For the junction between floor and wall

ZOCLOPRAC is a profile that is manufactured in flexible PVC, with a width of 2.76", available in 6 colors to combinate or contrast with the color of the wall where it is being installed.

It is placed it the union between wall and floor, using contact adhesive.

With its use, the section of the wall that meets with the floor, is protected against damages and wetness.

It comes in rolls of 82 feet, and its installation is fast and easy.
