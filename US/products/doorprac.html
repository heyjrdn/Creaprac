Doorprac

Door sweep

Covers the space between floor and door

The DOORPRAC is a profile that covers the gap between the door and floor, and prevents the entry of dust, insects, mosquitoes, streams of cold or hot air, small animals, and water runoff.

The DOORPRAC is manufactured with a coextrusion process, where they melt and bind profile PVC rigid and flexible PVC profile.

This way, the flexible profile that sweeps the floor when opening and closing the door, remains glued to the rigid profile that is adhered to the door, avoiding the separation of the two profiles with the passing of time, like in the case of the aluminum door sweep, where the aluminum profiles holds the flexible pvc profile by a compressing action, that may loosen up with time.

DOORPRAC comes with a unique feature, an opening and closing tap that hides the installation screws.

It is offered in 2 lengths, 2.94" and 3.92", and in 8 colors to combine or contrast with the color of the door.

The installation is easy: just measure the width of the door, choose the DOORPRAC in the appropriate length that exceeds the width of the door, cut it to that width, open the closing tap of the DOORPRAC starting in a corner, distribute 4 or 5 holes on the length of the wall of the rigid profile that remains in the back of the open tap, place it on the door, make sure the flexible profile rests over the floor but with out exaggerating the pressure over it, fasten the DOORPRAC to the door by using screws with pointed head through the holes, close the tap starting in the corner and continuing until it is completely closed.
