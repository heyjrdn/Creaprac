Grapaprac-P

Flat staple for cables

Staple wires and keep them tidy

How many times where you want to watch the television or use the computer, or use a lamp, or use the phone, there is no connection available.

Taking the cables to those destinations where you want to use all of those articles may imply the necessity of breaking the wall to hide the cables, and repairing again the wall to return it to its original look. This may result in an expensive and impractical process.

An economic, practical and functional alternative is available and that is to fix the cable to the wall, using different products that will avoid having them loose all over the floor, avoiding with their use a bad image, and giving security by not being a hazard by being loose all over the floor.

GRAPAPRAC-P is a steel flat staple that is use to fix cables to the wall so that they are not loose on the floor.

It is offer in 2 sizes, 9mm and 8mm in white color, and is ideal for interior or exterior applications.

It comes in a bag with 100 pieces.
