Cubreprac

Cover steel and cylindrical wire fences

Gives privacy and security to your property

CUBREPRAC is a plastic strip manufactured with a combination of high and low density poliethylene, with additives against UV rays and antioxidants for a lasting life on the outside.

With its use, sewing the strip through the holes of the cylindrical wire fence in a diagonal form, you give privacy and security to a property.

It can also be use in a vertical form for a steel fence that permits such installation.

It can be offer in different widths, ranging from 2" to 3", and in 8 different colors.
