Gomaprac

Rubber ring

With guide to install toilets

GOMAPRAC is a rubber ring with rigid pvc guide for toilet installation.

It has a unique rubber gum similar to a chewing gum for a perfect seal with the exhaust of the toilet, and a rigid pvc guide for a perfect fitting with the drain pipe of 4".

With this perfect seal and fit, the maximum hygene is obtained by avoiding leaks and bad odors.

The installation is simple and practical, since you only have to turn upside down the bowl of the toilet, place the GOMAPRAC over the exhaust of the toilet and when you turn the bowl again to set it over the drain pipe , the GOMAPRAC does not fall down, so it is very easy to set the rigid pvc guide over the drain pipe , and achieve a perfect fit, with no future leaks.

This can hardly be done with the traditional wax ring, since with this product you have to place first the wax ring over the drain pipe, and then set the bowl of the toilet over it, guessing the exact positon of the exhaust of the toilet to fit over the wax ring since the bowl by itself does not let you see what you are doing.
