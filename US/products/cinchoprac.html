Cinchoprac

Tie for cables

Holds cables and anything else

CINCHOPRAC is manufactured with nylon, and is use to hold several cables together, or to other parts, so that they are not loose and in complete disorder.

It can be use for outside or inside aplications on home, industry or office.

It is offer in 5 sizes, 4", 6", 8", 12", and 14" in white color.

It comes in a bag with 100 pieces.
