Canalprac

Duct profile for cables

Hides the cables and avoid breaking the wall

How many times where you want to watch the television or use the computer, or use a lamp, or use the phone, there is no connection available.

Taking the cables to those destinations where you want to use all of those articles may imply the necessity of breaking the wall to hide the cables, and repairing again the wall to return it to its original look. This may result in an expensive and impractical process.

An economic, practical and functional alternative is available and that is to fix the cable to the wall, using different products that will avoid having them loose all over the floor, avoiding with their use a bad image, and giving security by not being a hazard by being loose all over the floor.

CANALPRAC is a rigid pvc profile that is use to fix one or several cables to the walls, hiding them and maintaining them in perfect order.

This profile is composed of 2 rigid pvc profiles, one that is fixed to the wall by using screws and plastic anchors or by using the option of a double funtion adhesive tape that is adhered to the back of this profile and that when the protective red film of the tape is retrieved, the profile can be adhered to the wall.

The other rigid pvc profile serves as a tap on the profile that is fixed to the wall, hiding in the inside the cables.

CANALPRAC is offered in 4 sizes in white color, 10×20 mm, 11×22 mm, 25×16 mm and 30×15 mm; with the option of having the double function adhesive tape on them for an easy installation.

The size of 11×22 mm is offered with special pieces to be use during installations over inside or outside walls, to join two pieces of CANALPRAC horizontaly, to join an horizontal piece of CANALPRAC with a vertical piece, and as a tap to finish the end of the CANALPRAC.

In the other sizes, no special pieces are offered, so all the unions and bends during the installation, have to be done by cutting and fitting the CANALPRACS.

The size of 10×20 mm is offered in several colors besides white: black, oak, beige, grey and brown.

CANALPRAC can be ordered with double action tape (Cintaprac) to facilitate its installation and avoid the use of drill, screws and wall anchors.
