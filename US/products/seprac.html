Seprac

Spacer for floor and wall tiles

Allows that the grout to enter and seal at 100%

SEPRAC is manufactured in flexible PVC. With its use in the installation of floor and wall tiles, the minimum space between tiles is guarantee so that the grout can penetrate and seal with 100% assurance.

Due to the high temperature in the manufacturing process, the ceramic tile pieces have differences in dimensional size. By using SEPRACS, those dimensional differences are distributed in the space left in between tiles, by compressing and bending themselves in each of the extremities of their cross shape.

For the new rectified tiles, even though you may install them without any space between them, this is not recommended since by not having any separation between tiles, the grout will not penetrate at 100%, resulting in humidity filtration, with the corresponding disadvantages of mole formation.

The new size of SEPRACS in 1⁄32" and 1⁄16", are offered for rectified tiles. With their use, the minimum separation between tiles is achieved so that grout without sand, which is recommended for this size of SEPRACS, may penetrate and seal at 100%. They are offered with extra width for an easy removal before grouting.

For installations with SEPRACS of 1⁄8" to 1⁄2", use grouts with sand. Please remove them before grouting.

With the use of SEPRACS, the consumer and not the installer chooses the width of the grout for the job.
