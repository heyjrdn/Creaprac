Seprac NG

Spacer for floor and wall tiles

Allows that the grout to enter and seal at 100%

SEPRAC NG, is manufactured in flexible PVC and with its use in the installation of floor and wall tiles, the minimum space between tiles is guaranteed, so that the grout can penetrate and seal with 100% assurance.

Due to the high temperature in the manufacturing process, the ceramic tile pieces have differences in dimensional size. By using SEPRAC NG, those dimensional differences are distributed in the space left in between tiles, by compressing and bending themselves in each of the extremities of their cross shape.

They are manufactured in different sizes from 1⁄8" to 1⁄2", and they have the great advantage that it is not required to remove them before the grouting job is begun, since by being hollow, the grout can penetrate through them, therefore remaining under the grout.

It is recommended to use grouts with sand to avoid cracks in the grout.

With their use, the consumer and not the installer chooses the width of the grout for the job.
