Tiraprac L

L bullnose for floor and wall tiles

Finish the edge of tiles that meet in the corner of two walls

Ceramic tile does not have glaze on its edge, only on its surface.

TIRAPRAC L is a profile that has the same function as a TIRAPRAC, but with the peculiarity that the part that finishes the the edges of the tiles that cover the 90 degree corner of two meeting walls, is flat and not curved.

TIRAPRAC L is manufactured in rigid PVC in 18 solid colors, 17 marble colors, 3 wood colors and 6 aluminum finishes, giving you a lot of combination or contrast options to choose from.

It is offered in a length of 8" and is offered in 2 heights, 3⁄8" for most world wide tiles and 1⁄4" for some South American and Asian tiles.
