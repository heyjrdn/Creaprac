Mosaicprac

Glass mosaic

Decorate, cover and enhance environments

MOSAIPRAC is a glass, marble or stainless steel mosaic, or a combination of them, laid over a fabric mesh to facilitate its installation.

It is use to cover, decorate and detail spaces.

In the HP line, the glass mosaic is presented in 2 sizes: 23×23×4 mm. that is combined in a mesh of 298×298 mm. where each box contains 22 meshes that are equivalent to 1.95 m2; and 25×25×8mm that is combined in a mesh of 300×300mm where each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.08 m2.

In the STG line, the glass mosaic of size 15×50×8mm is combined with a marble mosaic of the same size in a mesh of 304×310mm where each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.13 m2.

In the Stainless Steel line, the stainless steel mosaic is presented in 2 sizes: 25×25×8 mm. that is combined in a mesh of 300×300mm and each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.08 m2; 23×48×8 mm. that is combined in a mesh of 298×298 mm. where each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.08 m2.

In the STQ line, the glass mosaic of size 15×50×8 mm. is combined with a marble mosaic of the same size and of size 15×15×8 mm., that is combined in a mesh of 304×310 mm. where each box contains 9 meshes that are equivalent to 0.85 m2.

In the K line, the glass mosaic of size 48×48×8 mm. is combined in a mesh of 300×300 mm. where each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.08 m2.

In the ST line, the glass mosaic of size 15×15×8 mm. is combined with a glass mosaic of the same size, and in the Burgos model, also with a stainless steel mosaic of the same size in a mesh of 305×305 mm. where each box contains 12 meshes that are equivalent to 1.12 m2.

All the mosaic lines, except Series K, can be used in areas with dampness as swimming pools, bathrooms and showers.
