Creaprac was established in 1989, offering products to facilitate the installation of ceramic, porcelain and stone coverings, in order to achieve impeccable and durable installations.

With that goal in mind, products for other projects have been incorporated, such as: products for the handling of wires, plastic tape to cover wire fences, glass mosaics to decorate and liven up spaces, borders that bring to life and enhance wall tiles, mirrors to decorate and embellish bathrooms, glass blocks to isolate spaces and a lot more innovative products.

Our company has one factory with 5 plastic extrusion lines, located in Guadalupe, Nuevo León, Mexico, and 3 sales warehouses situated in Monterrey, Guadalajara and Mexico City.

Our marketing is via distributors, mainly tile and hardware stores.

Thanks to the quality of our products, we successfully export to several destinations, including Central America, the Caribbean, South America and the United States of America.

In order to offer a better service to our customers in the United States, we have a sales warehouse located in San Diego, California.
