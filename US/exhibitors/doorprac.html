Doorprac
Exhibitor

Exhibitor of 15.74"

Exhibitor of 19.68"

Display that allows the Doorprac to be shown in both models, Doorprac and Doorprac NG.

It comes with a cardboard display that shows the 8 colors available and a physical sample of each one, and the advantages of its use.

Three of the sides of the box of the exhibitor are printed with illustrative drawings that show the animals, insects, mosquitoes, water spillings and air currents that can not enter to a house through the bottom of the door, once the Doorprac is use.

This exhibitor comes available in 2 widths:

  • 15.74" for Doorprac of 35.43".
  • 19.68" for Doorprac of 35.43" and 47.24".

The front of the exhibitor comes with 2 printed labels with the name and size of the product, so that by using a marker you can write the sales price that you have for this product in your store, making the product more attractive for the final customer.
